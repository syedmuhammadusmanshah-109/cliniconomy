Medical learning for MBBS students

Welcome to Cliniconomy

A student-led medical education platform built to simplify MBBS learning through structured clinical teaching, smart preparation tools, and a strong academic community.

Founded by Syed Muhammad Usman Shah, an MBBS student at Bacha Khan Medical College, Cliniconomy was created with a vision to make medical education more practical, organized, and student-focused.

The platform currently provides:

  • Clinical examination lectures
  • Structured MBBS learning resources
  • MCQ-based exam preparation
  • Academic guidance and community support

Our goal is not only to teach topics, but to help medical students understand concepts clinically, prepare confidently for exams, and grow throughout their MBBS journey.

Cliniconomy is starting with Upper and Lower Limb clinicals, but the long-term vision is to build a complete preparation workspace for MBBS students — combining lectures, exams, resources, planning tools, and community learning in one platform.

Learn smarter. Prepare better. Grow together.

— Cliniconomy
